About Resurgence

Resurgence is the annual cultural fest of Shri Mata Vashino Devi University. More than 2,000 Students participate directly or indirectly in this mega-event. Apart from the socio-cultural events different informal event fine arts, literary and fun events enhances the aroma of Fest. The Cycle began eleven years ago as more of an initiative than an effort to glamorize the potential.
